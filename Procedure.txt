Step 1: Chop the onions into small pieces.
Step 2: Pour some oil into a pan and then fry the onions and curry leaves.
Step 3: After 2-3 mins, add some turmeric, red chilli powder, and salt(as per taste).
Step 4: Sprinkle some Garam Masala and pepper(optional).
Step 5: Let them fry until the smell of the onions get mixed with the smell of the turmeric and salt.
Step 6: Break the eggs and put it in the pan.
Step 7: Stir well until the eggs get cooked and be soft enough to eat. It should not be too liquidy or fried much.
Step 8: Garnish with coriander.
